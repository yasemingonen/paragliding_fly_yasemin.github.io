About Me

Hi, I'm Paragliding Pilot Yasemin!


No experience is necessary!

Paragliding is as close to flying as you can get and a tandem flight puts you in the hot seat giving you the perfect chance to enjoy your first flight leaving the technicalities to your experienced pilot.

Oludeniz offers the best tandem paragliding site in Europe, if not the world. The spectacular views and stable weather conditions puts it right up there. From the perfect take off sites close to the 1960 m summit of Babadag Mountain, your flight will take you over the ridges with a birds eye view of the white streched beach and the famous lagoon. After a 25 to 45 minute flight, you will smoothly land close to the beach.
